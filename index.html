About

The woman
behind the lens

Born in Sana'a and raised between ancient alleyways and modern crossroads, Mariam discovered photography as a way to preserve the beauty she saw fading. Every click is a commitment — to light, to truth, to the untold stories of Yemen.

Her work spans the intimate and the epic: from quiet wedding portraits in candlelit halls to vast desert landscapes that have remained unchanged for centuries. She shoots with available light, natural frames, and an unshakeable belief that the best photographs are the ones that feel inevitable.

"I don't take photos. I collect moments that refuse to be forgotten."
